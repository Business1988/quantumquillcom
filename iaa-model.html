IAA Model

IAA monetization should behave like an editorial decision: placed with timing, not sprayed everywhere.

QuantumQuillCom frames ad-supported revenue as a sequence of choices about interruption, trust, cadence, and long-term tolerance. The goal is not only revenue lift. The goal is a business model that does not flatten the software's character.

Placement pacing

Interruptions should follow natural pauses, not force them.

Reward logic

Value exchanges must feel understandable rather than extractive.

Retention awareness

Revenue works longer when the product remains usable.

Public legitimacy

Support and privacy pages help contextualize the monetized experience.

Monetization NoteGood IAA monetization respects the reading speed of a session.

That principle shapes how QuantumQuillCom positions revenue within the mobile product loop.
